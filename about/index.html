About me

Welcome to my website. My name is Ásgeir Berg (IPA: [auːskeir̥]) and I’m a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University of Iceland’s Institute of Philosophy.

I have a PhD in Philosophy from the University of St Andrews and the University of Stirling (SASP) and was formerly a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher at Stirling.

My current research interests are in the philosophy of mathematics and issues related to rule-following.

Please check out my CV or email me here. You can access my papers, published or otherwise, under Research.

If you are looking for my thesis on Wittgenstein’s remarks on Gödel, it can be found here.
