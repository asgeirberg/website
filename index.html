About

I'm an Icelandic philosopher working on metasemantics and the philosophy of mathematics. I'm currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Iceland's Institute of Philosophy.

Previously, I was a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher at the University of Stirling. In 2020, I received my PhD in Philosophy jointly from Stirling and St Andrews (SASP).

You can find my papers under Research. If you are looking for my thesis on Wittgenstein's remarks on Gödel, it can be found here.
