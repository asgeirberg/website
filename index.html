Welcome to my website. My name is Ásgeir (IPA: [auːskeir̥]) and I was until recently a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher at the University of Stirling. I’m currently a PhD student in Philosophy there and at the University of St Andrews (SASP).

I also have an M.Sc in Logic from the University of Amsterdam.

I work on Wittgenstein and the philosophy of mathematics. I’m also interested in the history of analytic philosophy, logic and semantic paradoxes.
